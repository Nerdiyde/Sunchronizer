How does the calculator work? ▼

Calculation Method:

  1. System Capacity: Number of modules × Power per module = Total capacity (kW)
  2. Annual Generation: Irradiance × System capacity × 365 days × System efficiency × Tracking bonus
  3. Module Degradation: Each year, panels lose 0.5% (default) efficiency due to aging
  4. Inflation Adjustment: Electricity prices increase 2% annually (default)
  5. Annual Savings: Generated energy × Electricity price - Annual maintenance costs
  6. Payback Period: Total investment ÷ Annual savings (Year 1)
  7. 30-Year ROI: (Total savings - Investment) ÷ Investment × 100%
  8. CO₂ Avoidance: Generated energy × 0.4 kg CO₂/kWh (European average)

Comparison: The tracker's yield is compared against a fixed panel system with the same capacity, considering orientation and tilt angle penalties.
